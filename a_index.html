Sanothimi Campus, officially known as Tribhuvan University Sanothimi Education Campus, is one of the oldest and most prestigious education campuses in Nepal. Established in 2023 B.S. (1966 A.D.), it was founded under the Ministry of Education to provide professional teacher training and quality education in the field of education and pedagogy.

Located in Bhaktapur, Nepal, Sanothimi Campus played a pioneering role in shaping Nepal's education system by preparing thousands of competent educators and academic leaders. Initially started with a focus on Education and Teaching Training Programs, the campus later became a constituent campus of Tribhuvan University, Nepal's oldest and largest university.

Over the years, Sanothimi Campus expanded its academic offerings to include programs like:

  • Bachelor in Information Communication Technology Education (BICTE)
  • Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)
  • Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) in various subjects
  • Master of Education (M.Ed.)
  • Post Graduate Diploma in Education (PGDE)
  • Special Need Education (SNE)

The campus is renowned for its highly experienced faculty, strong academic environment, and dedication to excellence in teaching, learning, and research.

With a rich legacy of over five decades, Sanothimi Campus continues to be a center of academic distinction and a beacon for aspiring educators across the country.
